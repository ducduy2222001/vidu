---Flex-flow Shorthand---
- Thuộc tính flex-Flow là một shorthand cho flex-direction và flex-wrap. Chẳng hạn, bạn có thể sử dụng:
flex-direction: column;
flex-wrap: wrap;
---Ghi tắc---
flex-flow: column wrap;

flex-direction: row

- Trục chính chạy từ trái sang phải (mặc định)

1
2
3
4

flex-direction: row-reverse

- Trục chính chạy từ phải sang trái

1
2
3
4

flex-direction: column

– Trục chính chạy từ trên xuống dưới

1
2
3
4

flex-direction: column-reverse

– Trục chính chạy từ dưới lên trên

1
2
3
4
